What should I get my house soft washed?

Over the course of a house's lifetime, organic buildup naturally occurs. This can lead to damage down the line if left untreated especially on roofs, shingles, and exteriors. At GruntWorx, we will leave your house clean and like new every time.

What is the difference between soft washing and pressure washing? What is right for me?

Soft washing uses chemicals (most often Sodium Hypochloride) to remove organic buildup from your exteriors. Pressure washing on the other hand uses water pressure to physically remove stains and build up. A common rule is to save pressure washing for concrete and other hardscapes while exteriors and vinyl should be soft washed to protect them from damage.
